How Piecemeal works

flash_on

Quick & Easy to Join a Bill

Everyone can join the same bill from their own phones by going to piecemeal.com and entering the unique code for this bill. No login or downloading required.

group

Add or Share Dishes

Add the dishes you ordered and share dishes with others. Tax and tip are divided proportionally. Everyone gets an itemized total.

settings

Easy Bill Split & Payment

Once the bill is split, automatic payment requests can optionally be sent through Venmo. Plus, if you use Piecemeal frequently, you can stay logged in through Venmo.
